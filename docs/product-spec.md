**Status:** 🚧 In Development

# Overview

FitMate AI is an AI-powered fitness and nutrition companion designed to simplify healthy living through intelligent automation. The application combines nutrition tracking, computer vision, personalized recommendations, and fitness analytics into a single mobile experience.

Rather than manually calculating macros or maintaining spreadsheets, users can simply log meals, upload food images, and receive AI-generated nutritional insights while tracking long-term health goals.

---

# The Problem

Maintaining a healthy lifestyle is difficult due to inconsistent meal tracking, lack of nutritional awareness, and the complexity of calculating daily macro requirements.

Many beginners struggle to answer questions like:

- How much protein should I consume?
- Am I eating enough fiber?
- How many calories are in this meal?
- How much water should I drink?
- How can I recover after over-eating during travel?

Existing calorie trackers require excessive manual input and fail to provide intelligent guidance.

---

# Goals

- Simplify nutrition tracking with AI.
- Reduce manual meal logging.
- Help beginners understand fitness fundamentals.
- Build sustainable habits through smart insights.
- Create an intelligent digital fitness companion rather than a simple calorie tracker.

---

# Solution

FitMate AI combines artificial intelligence with mobile technology to automate nutrition tracking and provide personalized health recommendations.

Users can log meals manually or upload food images for AI-powered nutritional analysis. The platform tracks daily macro intake, hydration, supplements, and calories while generating personalized recommendations based on individual goals and lifestyle.

## Recommendation for a production-quality build

Rather than using **Firebase + Supabase + PostgreSQL together**, keep the architecture simpler:

- **Flutter** + Riverpod + GoRouter
- **NestJS (TypeScript)** backend
- **PostgreSQL** with **Prisma ORM**
- **Redis** for caching and rate limiting
- **Cloud Storage** for meal images
- **Gemini Vision/OpenAI Vision** for food recognition
- **OpenAI/Gemini LLM** for coaching and diet assistant
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)** only for push notifications
- **Docker + CI/CD + environment-based configs** for deployment

This architecture is easier to maintain, scales well, and is strong enough for a real-world SaaS product with hundreds of thousands of users.

Overall, your phased strategy—**Emergent AI for the complete frontend foundation and Antigravity for incremental full-stack feature implementation—is an excellent way to build FitMate without overwhelming either AI tool while keeping the project modular and production-ready.**
